## Epic goal

Turn CoreQuote outputs into workshop-ready production handoff material that reduces mistakes in cutting, edging, picking, and manufacturing.

## Candidate capabilities

- Cutlists grouped by board, thickness, material, and section.
- Edging summary.
- Grain direction and rotation guidance.
- Part labels.
- Board requirement totals and waste allowance.
- CSV/XLSX export for downstream tools.
- Future cut optimization or integration with a cut optimizer.

## Placeholder acceptance direction

This phase should be scoped with real workshop feedback after Phase 2 exports exist and can be reviewed by someone who cuts and assembles cabinetry.
