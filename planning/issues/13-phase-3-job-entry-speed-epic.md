## Epic goal

Make it fast to enter a full kitchen, wardrobe, vanity, or built-in quote without creating every unit one at a time.

## Candidate capabilities

- Duplicate units.
- Duplicate quotes.
- Bulk add units in a spreadsheet-like grid.
- Reorder units.
- Group units by room, run, area, or installation zone.
- Apply board, handle, slide, hinge, height, and depth defaults to many units.
- Add common job templates for kitchens, wardrobes, vanities, and built-ins.

## Placeholder acceptance direction

This phase should not be broken down until Phase 1 and Phase 2 outputs are usable. When scoped, every issue should include a real quote-entry scenario and a measurable time-saving goal.
