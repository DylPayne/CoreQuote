## Epic goal

Support the business workflow around quoting: clients, sending, approval, deposits, users, permissions, support, and operational records.

## Candidate capabilities

- Client/contact management.
- Quote email/send flow.
- Public quote link.
- Accept, reject, or request changes.
- Signature or approval capture.
- Deposit/payment tracking.
- Activity history.
- User invitations and roles.
- Company branding, data export, backup, billing, and support settings.

## Placeholder acceptance direction

This phase should be broken down once CoreQuote can reliably produce quote and workshop outputs that a business would send and use.
